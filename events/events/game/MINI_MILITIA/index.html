RULES

1. Best of 5 rounds. 7:30 min each. 10 min for last round. Maps in order of playing
a. SNOW BLIND
. SUBDIVISION
c. CATACOMBS
d. HIGH TOWER
e. OUTPOST
First 4 rounds will be won by the team with greater number of kills, while the last round will be won by the team who have more score points [i.e. kills – death].
2. Each team can have maximum 5 player and minimum 3 player.No changing of player is allowed
once the names are registered. And all the player of team will play all the rounds. If any rule is
violated team will lose that round.
3. Once the winner is decided no further round/s (if left) will be played.
4. Player repetition is not allowed. If a player is found in more than one team then he/she will be
asked to choose any one team and the other team will have to play with (N-1) player. If (N-1 to 3)
then the team will not play. In case the team with less no. of player don’t want to play/not
allowed to play, no refund will be awarded.
5. Players can bring any device of their choise but the game installed in it should not be modified or
any upgrade due to level up shall not be present. In case any type of cheating is found the whole
team will be disqualified with no refund.
6. All the avatars should be customized and no default avatar will be allowed. NOOBIE is not allowed as player name.
7. IN CASE OF CONNECTIVITY LOSS DUE TO ANY REASON, THE TEAM RESPONSIBLE FOR SUCH
ACT WILL LOOSE THAT ROUND OF MATCH.
8. Organising committee holds the right to dismiss the tournament at any time and in such case
registration fees will be refunded. All the decision made by committee will be final.
