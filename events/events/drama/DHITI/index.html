RULES

1. Participants: 8-11 members per team (+2 for backstage lights, sound, etc)
2. Time limit - 30 (+5) minutes
3. Language : English/Hindi
4. Teams to pre register before 12th of February 2017
5. Microphones, backstage mics and laptops for playing music/tracks will be provided
6. In case of technical failure, teams are allowed to start from the last scene
7. Props: 5 chairs and 2 tables will be provided. Any other props should be brought by the team.
8. Any props that damage the stage/ injure other people or damage the stage will not be
allowed (fire, smoke, etc)
9. Teams must arrange their own costumes. The organisers are not responsible for any lost
equipment/ costumes
10. Misconduct or vulgarity will result in immediate disqualification
11. Judges’ decision is final and cannot be contested
Registration - Rs. 1000
